Chef Cheoffrey's Restaurant Run

3v1 multiplayer game designed and implemented from the ground up

The goal of UC San Diego's CSE 125 course is to design and implement a real-time, 3D, multiplayer video game from scratch, without using any game engine like Unity--all within 10 weeks.

Restaurant Run is a one versus three player game. The chef just finished making a cake, but three animals have decided to take that cake for themselves!

My team, Salt Shaker Games, is a team of six:

Athena Hosek, Hung Tran, Kate Wong, Sterling Christensen, Richard Du, and myself.

Music by: Andy Meza.
