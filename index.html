Facilitator

Over the course of a year, taught K-12 students in underprivileged areas skills in Scratch, TinkerCAD, and SonicPi. Each session would involve 1 hour sessions developing a certain skill in each of the respective technologies. Usually this would include a fun task, such as learning how to model a 3D boat in TinkerCAD, then testing that model the following week - or completing a challenge game in Scratch. I wanted to teach these kids to help empower them with creative problem solving skills and introduce them to technology that would otherwise be easily accessible in these areas. While teaching, I learned valuable speaking skills as well as how to properly convey high level topics in a simple and concise way such that a 2nd to 8th grader could understand.
